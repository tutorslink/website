Platform Policies

For Tutors +

  • Hide your identity until payment is made.
  • Do not share contact info with students before payment.
  • Accept 40% commission of first month fees.
  • Must be at least 13 years old.

For Students +

  • Pay 100% before connecting with tutor.
  • Do not share contact info before payment.
  • Fees held by Tutors Link first month, then transferred to tutor.
  • Prices per class fixed by tutor.

General +

  • Tutors Link is responsible for issues in the first month.
  • Not responsible for issues after first month.
  • Breaking policies can result in removal.

Refunds +

  • Refunds only in first month.
  • Case 1: After demo class, full refund if issues arise.
  • Case 2: During first week, refund for classes not attended or issues proven.
